🇮🇳 About Us

Documents, sorted — without giving up your privacy.

DocStitcher is a free, browser-based document toolkit built in India for everyday paperwork — Aadhaar, PAN, driving licence, passport photos, and everything in between.

DocStitcher started with a simple, annoying problem. Government and bank portals across India ask for a specific thing: front and back of a document, stitched into a single A4 PDF, under a certain file size. Doing that with a phone camera usually means downloading three different apps, none of which are free, all of which want your files on their server, and most of which are impossible to skip past for a one-off Aadhaar upload.

So we built the tool we wished existed — one page, every common document task, and nothing sent anywhere it doesn't need to go. Document Stitcher combines your front and back scans into a clean A4 PDF. The Passport & ID Photo Maker crops and sizes your photo to exact specs and tiles multiple copies on one sheet. The AI Background Remover cleans up photo backgrounds using a model that runs on your own device. And the Single-Doc Tools handle cropping, rotating, compressing, converting, and watermarking — all before a file ever reaches the stitcher.

Everything is processed with JavaScript and the Canvas API, directly inside your browser tab. There's no backend that receives your files, no account to create, and no upload step for your documents. The only thing that needs the internet is the app itself and the ads that keep it free.

What we believe

A few principles guide every feature we build.

🔒
Your files stay yours
Cropping, PDF creation, compression, and background removal all happen inside your browser. We have no server that stores or even briefly touches your documents.
🆓
Free, with no catch
Every tool is free with no tiers, limits, or sign-up wall. DocStitcher stays afloat through ads, not by charging for basic document tasks people need occasionally.
🇮🇳
Built for Indian paperwork
Aadhaar, PAN, driving licence, and passport photo formats are baked into the presets — because we built this for the forms we actually have to fill out.
⚡
No friction
No installs, no account, no watching a progress bar upload your Aadhaar to a stranger's server. Open the app and the tool is already working.
What's inside

Document Stitcher

Passport & ID Photo Maker
